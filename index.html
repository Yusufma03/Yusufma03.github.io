Biography

I’m a fourth year PhD student at the School of Computing, National University of Singapore, under the supervision of Prof. David Hsu. I also work closely with Prof. Wee Sun Lee. My research mainly focuses on uncertainty modelling, reinforcement learning, graph neural networks, and their applications to robotics.

Prior to NUS, I received my bachelor’s degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China, under the supervision of Prof. Fan Wu and Prof. Xiaofeng Gao.
